# Air Purifier Market Fit Analytics

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes air quality (AQI), health impact, vehicle adoption, and population data in India
to evaluate market fit and product strategy for an air purifier startup.

The objective is to enable leadership teams to:
- Identify high-risk, high-demand cities
- Understand pollution-driven health impact
- Design product features aligned with regional pollution patterns
- Prioritize markets using data-driven insights

##  Business Use Case
India faces severe air pollution challenges, with multiple cities ranking among the most polluted globally.
This project supports strategic decisions for:
- Market entry prioritization
- Product feature definition
- Pricing and segmentation strategy
